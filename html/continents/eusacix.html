Eusacix

Eusacix is the continent where the main story is being told. It is a continent with a lot of diversity in terms of biomes, with a huge wasteland on the southeast, mega forests, 2 chains of mountains, several plains, and a tundra on the east.

The nations that are known to exist in Eusacix are:

  • The Holy Sovereignty of Dhavalon
  • The Erohttus Coalition
  • The Ghivernna Kingdom
  • The Hestein Empire
  • The Principality of Kahla'gar
  • The Krigdalthar Empire
  • The Lhanersk Palatinate
  • The Nyuldher Coalition of Marshlands
  • The Phenbora Dominion
  • The Solthios Wasteland
