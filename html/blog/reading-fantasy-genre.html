Reading Fantasy Genre

    The Purpose

    I was watching a Youtube video from Daniel Greene where he comes out with the best way to find your niche in the Fantasy Genre. I watched the entire video and like it a lot for several reasons. One of them is, well, the Recommendation, of course. But more importantly, the diferent ramifications that he goes from book to book. The only problem I had with it was that I couldn't zoom in on the diagram he wrote in his whiteboard, and he's instagram post wasn't good enough for me. So, I decided to recreate the entire thing by myself with code. I added the pretty colors, arrow comments, zoom functionality. And, It was done after two days of repeating the video again and again.
    Now, I have leave the thing mostly as original as it was, but I most likely will add more books and more niche subgenres at some point. Maybe...
    Will see what happens.

    The Graph

    You should obviously start at the Hobbit like Daniel Greene did, and guide yourself by the comments and the legend at the top.
    Enjoy:
