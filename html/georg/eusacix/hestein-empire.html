Hestein Empire

    Hestein

    The Hestein Empire is a relative new nation formed with 350 years of history. It was founded by Oswin Hestein also known as The Father Founder. This nation is divided in two regions, the northern region is known as Hesteinese Region, the southern region is known as the Lhumigian Region, and two special regions. And futhermore, each region is divided in several duchies.

    Duchies of the Hesteinese Region

    1. Duchy of Roseward
    2. Duchy of Wheattalon
    3. Duchy of Hartridge
    4. Duchy of Fallenoak

    Duchies of the Lhumigian Region

    1. Duchy of Spiderhorn
    2. Duchy of Amberguard
    3. Duchy of Timberborne
    4. Duchy of Stonedall

    Special Regions

    1. Matuk Principality
    2. Ulafir Principality
