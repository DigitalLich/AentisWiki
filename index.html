Land of Chaos and Balance

The mist and the rain of ashes have been running wild throught for thousands of years leaving the people to find thier own way to survive. Until 750 years ago, the sky open up for a brief moment of the day and the sun shined through the ashes signaling the end of the Era of Ashes marking what is now known as the Era of Equilibrium. People from all the world looked at this a s hope and opportunity, and the race for power and control began. Nations started to rise, wars strated to brew, and conflict is still anew.

Aentis Is...

An epic fantasy setting with a focus of medievalish society and culture. It focused itself in to the politics of rising nations, the influence of several deities in the world, the consequences of appraoching an industrial revolution, and the discovery of ancient secrets sealed away for unknown reasons.
It's a world where it seemes that always is a tipping balance trying to kept itself in check between chaos and order but not good or evil. The world is constantly revolutionizing itself, and the people must adapt to it or be left behind.

Current State

The world has been in development for 4 years, and the lore is still being written until today. The world map has been been drawn at least in paper as well as other maps that you can find in the Continents section, and the nations are still being created respectively.

Several NPCs have been created for important roles and lorewise while mudane NPCs are still being developed while campaings are played and books are writen.
